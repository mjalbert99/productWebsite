About the Website

This website is product engineering idea website. You may be asking yourself what does that mean? Well it's a place for users to come and post product ideas they came up with. Explaining what's its purpose, the product layout, pricing, direct competitors, and other information related to the idea. Then the community can go through the ideas posted by others, rate them, and post feedback so the original poster can get a better feel for their idea and find out what other's think. These features are only available to registered members. So if you fancy yourself the creative type or want to help others better refine their ideas create an account and become a member of this wonderful community
