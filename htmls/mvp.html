MVP: Smart Auto Blinds

Smart auto blinds are a new designed system of blinds that use electro-polarizing film to act as a way to block or let in an automated or manually controlled way by the user. This system would allow a user to connect with their smartphones to the film allowing them to adjust the level of tint on the fly or set a specific level of light that should be allowed through the film at any time based on the current light levels of the outside environment.

Product Layout

  • Persona: People who work are wanting a more advanced system to adjust the amount of natural light that is allowed in through their windows. Customers who want to automate or be able to control the natural light in a room by a push of a button instead of manually have to pull and twist blind cords and rods to open or close their blinds.

  • Job to be Done: Provide an automated system that will adjust electric polarized blinds remotely or by a schedule, in order to allow a smart home experience for a user as well as bring to market a better system then manual blinds to have a wider range of light level a user could choose over old-fashioned blinds

  • Product Design: Variable electric polarized screen to replace traditional blinds. Can be used with app where it can be manually set or scheduled to let in a certain amount of light

  • Competitors: Traditional Blind Companies

  • Unique Value Proposition: Ease of use and smart use tech in a new setting. Allows a user to adjust to any light level a window would let in( traditional blinds don't have wide range but more of a on and off setting)

  • Revenue Model: Direct to customer/ Sold to distributors
